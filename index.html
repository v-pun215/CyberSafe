Staying safe online is essential to protect your personal information from theft and misuse. By practicing good cybersecurity habits, you safeguard yourself against scams, identity theft, and privacy breaches, ensuring a secure and worry-free online experience.

Steps

These are some steps that should be taken to maintain cybersecurity whilst online.

Use strong passwords.

Use antivirus software.

Check links before you click.

Avoid public networks.
